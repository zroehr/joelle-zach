The Resort

We have blocked off some rooms in every room category. There are balcony rooms facing pool/garden or the ocean, swim-up rooms with a hammocks on the patios and over each room’s private pool, two-story rooms with a pool on its private roof, family rooms with a connecting bathroom and family rooms with a private pool. In addition to the standard buildings, all of these rooms can also be found in the Finest Club and Excellence Club, which have upgraded Bvlgari toiletries, upgraded liquor bottles in rooms, private check-in, private bistro restaurant, heated pools in winter, etc. The only difference between Finest Club and Excellence Club is that Finest Club offers luxury to guests of all ages, while Excellence Club is adult only. All the rooms, including those that are not in Finest Club or Excellence Club, are beautiful and start at 900 sq. ft., so we have faith that you will be happy with whichever room you choose!

The resort has 13+ dining options, 13 bars, a spa, cigar lounge, a golf course, daily activities and a kids club, so there is something for everyone.
